Report:¶

The next 3 research questions all use a two sample T-test to evaluate the means of sales between car manufacturers and vehicle type. The goal is to compare the means to see how vehicle type and manufacturer effect sales. The report will also evalutate the means between the vehicle type and fuel efficiency. All of the questions are answered from Brinich csv 1 and Brinich csv 2 files.¶

1. Does vehicle type affect sales in thousands?¶

In [24]:
StatTests.MeansTest(finaldf,'Type','Sales_in_thousands')

The groups are Passenger and Car

This is a test of equal variances with Ho: The variances are equal
The t test statistic is 16.956 and the p-value is 0.0
Conclusion: Reject Ho: The variances are not equal

This is a Welch (unequal variances) Two-Sample t test of equal means with Ho: The group means are equal
The t test statistic is -2.493 and the p-value is 0.0278
Conclusion: Reject Ho: The means are not equal

Conclusion: The P-value is less than 0.05, therefore we reject the null hypothesis that there is a no different between vehicle type and sales in thousands. In layman's terms, there is a an affect of vehicle type and sales as cars have a significantly more amount of sales.¶
